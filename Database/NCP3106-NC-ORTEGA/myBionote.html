A Student's Bio Note

Nicolle Clarisse Ortega

Nicolle Clarisse Ortega is a student from University of the East-Manila and currently taking up Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering. She is now in her 3rd year. In her 1st year, she became one of the Top 5 Most Outstanding Student in her batch. In her 2nd year, she became the Engineering Student Council - Secretary. And now in her 3rd year, she is the Vice President for Audit in Society of Computer Engineering Students. She is also one of the grantees of CHED Scholarship.

"In the end, we only REGRET the chances we didn't TAKE"

Nicolle grew up in Candaba, Pampanga. In her primary education, she graduated from Maguinao, Elementary School with special awards. In her Junior High School, she graduated from Carlos F. Gonzales High School, and in her Senior High School, she graduated from Polytechnic University of the Philippines with honors. After her college graduation, Nicolle still plans to pursue her dream to become a flight attendant.
